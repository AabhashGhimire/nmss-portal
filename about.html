About the Science Club

A space where students learn to think, question, and grow through science.

Who we are

The NMSS Science Club is an academic initiative of Narayani Model Secondary School. It supports students beyond textbooks by encouraging curiosity, logical thinking, and a deeper understanding of scientific concepts.

What we do

The club organizes activities, events, exhibitions, and discussions that allow students to explore science in a practical and engaging way.

Why this platform exists

This platform brings notices, events, certificates, and updates into one place for easy access and clear communication.

Our focus

We focus on inclusive learning, student growth, and building confidence in scientific thinking.
